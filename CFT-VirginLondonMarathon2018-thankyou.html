Thank you

Thank you for donating to our London Marathon team. Your support will bring us closer to our goal of beating cystic fibrosis for good. Through investing in cutting-edge research, driving up standards of care, offering support for everyone affected by cystic fibrosis and by campaigning hard and shouting loud about the issues that matter, we will get there. To find out more about the work we do please click here

If you would like to support our marathon runners on the 22nd April, we would love you to come along to one of our cheer points on the day. We can give you a brilliant cheer pack so you can be seen and heard by our runners! To join us, please email londonmarathon@cysticfibrosis.org.uk.
